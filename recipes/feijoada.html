Feijoada

A bean stew with meat recipe

Feijoada originates from Portuguese-speaking countries and regions.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups black beans (soaked overnight)
  • 1/2 lb pork (chopped)
  • 1 sausage (sliced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook the black beans in water with bay leaf until tender.
  2. In a separate pan, fry the pork and sausage until browned.
  3. Add onion and garlic, saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Combine the meats with the cooked beans.
  5. Simmer for 20 minutes, season with salt and pepper.
  6. Serve with rice and orange slices.
