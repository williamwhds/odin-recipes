Tropeiro Beans

A traditional brazilian bean and crispy pork crackling recipe

This is a special plate from Minas Gerais. Learn how to make this delicious dish right now!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked pinto beans
  • 1 cup cassava flour (farofa)
  • 2 sausages (sliced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Fry the sausage in a pan until browned.
  2. Add onion and garlic, saut√© until softened.
  3. Stir in the cooked beans.
  4. Scramble the eggs in the mixture.
  5. Add cassava flour and season with salt and pepper.
  6. Mix well and serve.
