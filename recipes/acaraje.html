Acarajé

A bean fritter recipe

Acarajé is a delicious dish originally from African and Afro-Brazilian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup black-eyed peas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Salt to taste
  • Palm oil (for frying)
  • Shrimp (optional, for filling)

Steps

  1. Drain and peel the soaked black-eyed peas.
  2. Blend the peas with onion, garlic and salt until smooth.
  3. Heat palm oil in a deep pan.
  4. Shape the batter into small balls and fry until golden.
  5. Serve with shrimp or your preferred filling.
